Financial Cybersecurity Awareness

A web project designed to educate users on protecting their financial accounts online.

By Pravada Raval

This project delivers a sleek and responsive web experience focused on empowering bank customers with essential cybersecurity knowledge. It covers vital practices such as strong authentication, phishing detection, and secure digital interactions to ensure safer financial activities online.

🔐 Strong Passwords

Use complex passwords with letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid reusing passwords across accounts.

📲 Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security by enabling OTP or authentication apps.

👀 Monitor Accounts

Check transactions regularly and report suspicious activity immediately.

⚠️ Avoid Phishing

Do not click unknown links or emails pretending to be your bank.

🌐 Secure Networks

Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking. Use private and secure connections.

🚪 Log Out

Always log out after using financial accounts, especially on shared devices.
